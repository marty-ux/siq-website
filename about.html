The Founder

Marty Berman, Founder & CEO

Scalient IQ was founded by Marty Berman after 20+ years building and leading revenue across advertising, digital media, and technology. Marty has sold and led partnerships with CBS Sportsline, NBC, and major sports and entertainment brands; co-founded a digital transformation consultancy; and worked at the intersection of AdTech, MarTech, enterprise partnerships, sports, gaming, and Web3.

Across every one of those roles, the same problem kept showing up: revenue teams drowning in tools, reports, and sync meetings, yet still unable to answer simple questions like "Which accounts are actually in-market this week?" or "Which partner is truly moving the needle?"

Marty built Scalient IQ to close that gap — to turn GTM complexity into an actionable, orchestration-driven system that revenue teams can actually trust and operate. Not a vendor pitch. A practitioner's tool.

When you work with Scalient IQ, you're not buying a static playbook or a generic RevOps audit. You're partnering with someone who can think like a CRO, talk like a CMO, and build like a RevOps lead — then wire everything into a GTM Signal OS your team can actually run.
