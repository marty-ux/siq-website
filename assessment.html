The Process

Five Phases. Thirty Days.

Agents do the heavy lifting. Humans validate every finding before it ships.

01
Days 1–3

Discovery & Connection

30-minute kickoff call to understand your stack, team structure, GTM motion, and pain points. You authorize read-only OAuth connections to your CRM (HubSpot or Salesforce), intent platforms (Bombora, 6sense, G2), conversation intelligence (Gong, Chorus), marketing automation, and sales engagement tools. Standard flow. Same thing your team does to connect any tool to your CRM.

02
Days 3–7

Data Ingestion & Signal Mapping

SiQ agents pull and map your signal data. Trace how data moves (or doesn't) between systems. Assess record completeness, freshness, and duplication. Cross-reference contacts against accounts to identify missing buying committee personas. A human consultant doing this manually would take 2–3 weeks. Agents finish in days.

03
Days 7–21

Analysis & Scoring

Six core analyses run in parallel: Signal Health Audit, Buying Committee Mapping, Intent Pattern Analysis, CRM Integrity Report, Revenue Opportunity Score, Tech Stack Efficiency Rating. HITL checkpoint: a human reviews every finding before it goes into the report. Agents surface patterns. We validate and contextualize.

04
Days 21–25

Report Build

The Signal Health Report comes together. Executive summary that fits on one page (shareable with your leadership). Detailed findings per analysis area. Signal flow visualizations. Scored benchmarks vs. similar GTM orgs. Priority issues ranked by revenue impact. The report stands alone — no pitch deck attached.

05
Days 25–30

Readout & Next Steps

60-minute readout with your GTM leadership (VP Sales, VP Marketing, RevOps). Walk through findings, answer questions, discuss implications. If you want to act on the findings, that's the GTM Action Plan conversation — what you get when you move forward with SiQ Cortex. No pressure. The report has standalone value.
